LEGEND

The Below Are The Top 10 Superstars Of Soccer

1.Lionel Messi

This Argentine striker of Barcelona is often considered as one of the greatest players of all time. The lone player in history to possess five Ballon d’Ors. This 5’9″ is probably the finest dribblers of all time.

2.Cristiano Ronaldo

The only player to compete with Messi over the last decade, the Ballon d’Or this year, the goal-machine, the beauty, it’s him – Cristiano Ronaldo, the Portuguese,forward for Serie A club Juventus and Real Madrid.

3.Neymar Jr

One of the best players in the world, Neymar currently plays in the Ligue 1 for the club Paris Saint-Germain. Neymar is the second-highest goalscorer for the country, just behind Pelé with 64 goals in 103 matches.

4.Robert Lewandowski

The Polish professional footballer currently plays for Bayern Munich and is the captain of the Poland national team. He is popular among fans for his positioning, technique and finishing.

5.Kylian Mbappe

The Belgian footballer impressed everyone in the Premier League playing for the club Manchester City. He is considered one of the best players in the world with all the abilities to be the best midfielder.

6.Kevin De Bruyne

The French professional footballer is currently for Ligue 1 club Paris Saint-Germain and the France national team.He is known for his finishing, dribbling, and speed. He played 100 matches for PSG with over 100 goals.

7.Virgil van Dijk

The Dutch professional footballer, Virgil van Dijk, plays for English Premier League club Liverpool and captains the Netherlands national team.One of the best defenders in the world missed the Ballon d’Or.

8. Sadio Mane

Mané is a Senegalese footballer who plays for Premier League club Liverpool and the Senegal national team. He is one of the best footballers in the world won the 2018-19’s Premier League Golden Boot.

9. Erling Haaland

Borussia Dortmund striker is known for his pace, flexibility, and strength. He is one of the best young footballers in the world right now.He won the Golden Boot with nine goals in a match in the 2019 FIFA U-20 World Cup.

10. Mohamed Salah

The professional Egyptian footballer currently plays for Premier League club Liverpool and the Egypt national team he jiont Liverpool in2017. He is known for his calmness, finishing, dribbling, and speed.
